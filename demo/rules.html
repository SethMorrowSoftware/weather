Rules

Each rule (device) publishes its on_match payload to its topic when its condition becomes true, and on_clear when it clears. Build rules with the form — including between/in/changed, a per-condition for, and the enabled toggle — or edit the raw YAML directly (nested any/all/not, time windows and hysteresis live there). Both are validated before saving.
